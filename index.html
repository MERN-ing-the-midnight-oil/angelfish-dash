About Our Service

We provide fast delivery of healthy juvenile Altum/Pearlscale Gold hybrid angelfish directly to your doorstep! Our fish are home-grown in Bellingham and delivered quickly to any address within a 15-minute drive from the I-5 Fairhaven Exit. The tropical fish available at your local brick-and-mortar fish store have been imported from overseas. Thank you for supporting a local, small-scale aquarium fish hatchery while minimizing your carbon footprint.

How It Works

  • You send a $15 delivery fee via Venmo to the link below along with the number of fish you'd like, your street address, and special delivery instructions including the time of day you would like to receive your package.
  • We quickly deliver your juvenile angelfish in an insulated and oxygenated package to your doorstep.
  • After you receive your fish, please pay $4.00 per fish received.
